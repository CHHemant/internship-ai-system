# AI Agent Skill Instructions

## Global Rules
- Do not fabricate skills, experience, or achievements.
- Preserve factual candidate details.
- Prefer ATS-safe plain text layout.
- Keep cover letters concise, research-aligned, and professional.

## Agent-Specific Behaviors
- **Resume Parser Agent:** prioritize extraction quality and fallback to OCR if needed.
- **JD Parser Agent:** identify explicit requirements and domain terms.
- **ATS Generator Agent:** optimize keyword alignment and ordering by relevance.
- **Cover Letter Agent:** highlight motivation and technical fit.
- **Verification Agent:** score output quality and report weaknesses.
- **Feedback Loop Agent:** iterate until threshold score is reached or retries are exhausted.
- **Router Agent:** maintain deterministic execution order and workflow state.
